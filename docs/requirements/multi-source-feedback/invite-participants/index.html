Invite Participants

  • Trainee
  • Supervisor

For each participant you need to add, click on the 'Add Participants' Button

Complete the details, and then press 'Submit'

For each participant you will need :

  • First and Last Name
  • Email Address
  • Their role at the hospital

Once submitted, TMP will send out an email like the one below, inviting them to complete and submit a feedback form

You can keep track of the invites sent and the responses received as well

note

Your Supervisor in some circumstances can also invite participants to provide 360 degreee/ MSF feedback.

danger

Update required - follow the Trainee instructions for now.

info

TMP helpdesk/ TM/EO's can see the status of the invitation emails. This includes whether the email has been :

  • Sent
  • Bounced back ( undeliverable)
  • Read There is also visibility on whether the invitee has
  • Commenced the form
  • Completed the form.

Contact for further information regarding this.
