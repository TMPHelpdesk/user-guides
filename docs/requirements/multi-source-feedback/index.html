Multi-Source Feedback / 360 Degree Evaluations

caution

INCOMPLETE

Multi-Source Feedback / 360 Degree Evaluations are a tool to assess how the trainee is perceived by their fellow unit staff during a rotation.

Process​

The process to complete a multi source feedback assessment:

  • Trainee to add self-assessment and add participants. Participants will receive an email with a link to add feedback.
tip

Please allow time for the "Invites sent" field to update.

  • Supervisor will be able to review anonymised responses as they are added.
  • Supervisor and Trainee to conduct multi source feedback review to discuss feedback received.
  • Supervisor to allow Trainee to view the multi source feedback report
  • Trainee to add self-reflection after the review discussion.
  • Supervisor to add review and outcome.
info

Trainee self-reflection, Supervisor review and outcome will be automatically updated on the multi source feedback report. Once completed, the requirement can be submitted.

Access the requirement​

  • Trainee
  • Supervisor

Navigate to 'My Requirements' from the left hand menu, and then find the requirement which you wish start a multi-source feedback or 360 Assessment.

Click the down arrow to edit

Click the 'Open Multi Source Feedback' at the top right of the page.

Here you will find an overview of the multi source feedback process

EXTRA STEP HERE

Navigate to the 360 assessment/ multi source feedback requirement and click Open Multi Source Feedback

Here you will find an overview of the multi source feedback process. You can also see:

  • If the Trainee has completed their self assessment
  • The number of participants added and their contact details
  • The number of responses received from participants
