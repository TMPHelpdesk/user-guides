Prepare Report for Release

  • Trainee
  • Trainer
  • Supervisor
info

Your Supervisor will inform you when the report is ready to be released. Click on the Supervisor tab if you need to provide any guidance.

tip

Check the full status of your MSF/ 360 requirement with your TM/EO or the TMP Help desk - it can provide you with some next stpes to move on....

note

Not Applicable for Trainers

Review, revise or hide participant responses​

Find the response that requires revision or hiding. Click the down arrow to edit.

Here you can revise the response sent by a participant for the trainee's final report.

The Trainee will be able to see text from the 'Revised Response' in their feedback report.

You also have the option of hiding a particular response if it is unsuitable for the trainee to see

This will then update on the multi source feedback page.

Make report available for Trainee​

Once all the responses received are reviewed, make the report available for the trainee by clicking 'Allow trainee to view report' at the top of the page.

caution

The Trainee will not have access to the report until you release this report to them.
